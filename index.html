Greetings! I am a postdoc research associate in the Computer Science Department at the University of Iowa, working with Professor Tianbao Yang. I received my Ph.D degree in 2018 from Centre for Artificial Intelligent (CAI) in Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT), University of Technology Sydney, Australia (UTS), under the supervision of Professor Yi Yang in the ReLER Lab. My research interests include machine learning and its application to computer vision. In 2013, I received my Bachelor degree from Tianjin University, in Computer Science.
