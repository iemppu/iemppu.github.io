Brief biography
Before joining WSU, I was a postdoctoral research associate working with Professor Tianbao Yang at the Computer Science Department of University of Iowa. I received my Ph.D degree in 2018 from Centre for Artificial Intelligence (CAI) in Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT), University of Technology Sydney, Australia (UTS), under the supervision of Professor Yi Yang in the ReLER Lab. I was very fortunate to spend five years with people in the ReLER Lab. In 2013, I received my Bachelor degree from Tianjin University, in Computer Science.
